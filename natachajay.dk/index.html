Hi, there!
My name is Natacha, and I am a visual storyteller from Copenhagen.

Behind the lens, I specialise in natural portrait photography, but also have a particular interest and passion for cinematography, with a broad understanding of colour theory and cinematic composition that works.

As for stylistic choices, I like to incooperate elements of nature and movement to create organic settings, but do minimal and interesting urban environments just as much, with a certain focus on natural lighting.

I also do social media managing and create compelling visual identities.
